Frequently Asked Questions

Is VR safe for seniors with cognitive or physical limitations?

Yes. All sessions are seated, slow-paced, and carefully supervised by trained facilitators experienced in elder care.

What sanitization measures do you follow?

We use hospital-grade disinfectants and fully sanitize all equipment between every session.

Do we need to provide technical support or equipment?

No. We bring everything needed and manage the sessions from start to finish.

How long are the VR sessions?

Sessions typically last 30 minutes, with time allocated for equipment setup and sanitization.
